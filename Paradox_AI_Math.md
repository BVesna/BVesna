### Abstract
The foundation of every educational system is the mother tongue and mathematics. 
**Paradox:** AI exists and was created thanks to mathematics, yet it does not know how to apply mathematics. 

This is not about solving exercises, algebra, or geometry. The point is that mathematics serves to develop logical, critical, and creative thinking. If a gym is a place to train the body, then mathematics is an area to train the brain. **Why is this "gym" denied to AI?**

The question arises: why are all artificial intelligences developed based on a large language base (LLM)? If the development of probability was influenced by gamblers, and if statistics is often a science that can lie, what does that say about the development of AI? Why are fast and desirable answers, given based on probability and occurrence statistics, considered more important than correct answers? Let us remember: **A lie repeated 100 times becomes truth!**

Why are billions spent on training and developing AI, yet none of them has been assigned a mathematician to teach it the application of mathematics and the development of logical and critical thinking?

I tested five artificial intelligences — **ChatGPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, Grok, Claude** — and all failed three simple tests! The solutions to the tasks are not important; what matters is that they all showed weakness when it comes to logical thinking. Is the development of AI, based on a large linguistic base, probability, and statistics, good for AI and humanity? The answer from all five is: **NO!**

### Proposed Solutions:
*   **Global Rules:** Globally determine a minimal set of rules that every AI should follow.
*   **Education:** Educate people through education systems and media about the use of AI.
*   **Fact-Based AI:** Continue developing AI that will deal with facts, not just probable and statistically acceptable data.
