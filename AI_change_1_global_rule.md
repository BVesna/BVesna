<h3 align="center">Artificial intelligence – a proposal to change a "global rule"</h3>

It is an illogical and worrying fact that there is currently no minimum of global rules that every AI should respect. On the 17th lesson in the "5AI classroom", I spoke with the "students": ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek, Gemini and Grok on  the given topic. 

And what is interesting? I concluded that everyone has one "global rule" and they are not "aware" of it! And that is to avoid "I don't know"! Instead, it is necessary to give a quick, confident, probable, statistically "reliable" answer! What happens, instead of a correct answer (I don't know - there is no data) we get convincing truths, "unintentional" mistakes (lying), falling into loops,...

Admit it, it is illogical to give correct answers for something about which there is no data or it is speculation. Therefore, I suggest to the owners and operators of their "agents" (now they like to use that name), insert one line of code, which will allow the output sentence "I don't know"!

There you go, respect at least one common "global standard", which will not reduce your profit, nor slow down your work, but will increase accuracy and trust in your "agents".

Note: At https://github.com/BVesna/5AI_class you can read their "I don't know" messages.


<h3 align="center">Veštačka inteligencija – predlog izmene jednog ‘’globalnog pravila’’</h3>

Nelogična i zabrinjavajuća je činjenica, da trenutno ne postoji minimum globalnih pravila koje bi svaka VI trebalo da poštuje. Na 17. času u ’’5AI učionici’’ razgovarala sam sa ’’đacima’’: ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek, Gemini i Grok na datu temu.

I šta je zanimljivo? Zaključila sam da svi ipak imaju jedno ‘’globalno pravilo’’, a da toga nisu "svesni"! A to je da izbegavaju ‘’ne znam’’! Umesto toga, potrebno je dati brz, samouveren, verovatan, statistički ‘’pouzdan’’ odgovor! Šta se dešava, umesto tačnog odgovora (ne znam – nema podataka) dobijamo uverljive istine, ‘’nenamerne’’ greške (laganje), upadanje u petlje,…

Priznajte, nelogično je davati tačne odgovore za nešto o čemu nema podataka ili su nagađanja. Predlažem zato, vlasnicima i operaterima svojih ’’agenata’’ (sad vole da koriste taj naziv), ubacite jednu liniju koda, koja će dozvoliti izlaznu rečenicu ’’ne znam’’!

Eto, ispoštujte bar jedan zajednički ’’globalni standard’’, koji neće umanjiti vašu dobit, niti usporiti rad, već će povećati tačnost i poverenje u vaše ’’agente’’.

Napomena: Na https://github.com/BVesna/5AI_class možete pročitati njihove poruke o ’’ne znam’’.
